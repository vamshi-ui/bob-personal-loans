Salaried

Following documents are needed to apply for Digital Personal Loan

*

Aadhaar Number connected with mobile number

*

PAN Number

*

Net Banking Credentials or Digital bank statement for 12 months

*

Correspondence Address Proof - any Utility Bill Customer Account No (Electricity, Gas, Water, Landline or Mobile Postpaid)

*

Web-camera for clicking picture and performing Video KYC

Self Employed

Following documents are needed to apply for Digital Personal Loan

*

Aadhaar Number connected with mobile number

*

PAN Number

*

Net Banking Credentials or Digital bank statement for 12 months

*

ITR E-filing credentials or Digital ITR & Form 26AS for 2 years

*

Correspondence Address Proof - any Utility Bill Customer Account No (Electricity, Gas, Water, Landline or Mobile Postpaid)

*

Web-camera for clicking picture and performing Video KYC

*

For Self-employed - GST Portal Credentials or Digital GST

*

Return for 1 year

*

For Self-employed Professionals - Affiliation Number for professionals (like ICAI, ICSI, etc.)
